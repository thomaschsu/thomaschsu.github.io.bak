About

Tom is currently a student at Rutgers studying to become a full stack developer. He is an experienced Front-End Web Developer with a proven background in developing user-friendly websites and web applications. He is well-versed in various programming languages including JavaScript, HTML, CSS, Node, and more.

Tom graduated from Rutgers University in 2011 with a major in Information Technology. After graduation, he worked at MRA International for 5 years. He took a lead role in the production and maintenance of a front end Magento and WordPress company website. This included implementing a PunchOut catalog, systematically testing the website for bugs, and managing the backend Magento catalog. Tom was also responsible for building and managing graphics for the front end. He also provided client services in a massive company project that required imaging thousands of laptops and desktops for Elizabeth school districts all within tight time constraints.

On his free time, he likes to take care of his dog, drink, play video games, watch movies / tv shows / anime, and exercise. He is knowledgeable in headphones - music has always been a big part of his life. He currently owns the Fostex TH900, Focal Elear, and Sennheiser HD 58X Jubilee headphones. His “go-to” earbud is the RHA T20 IEM's, but also has the Tin Audio T2. His AMP and DAC of choice are the Burson Soloist and Schiit BiFrost Multibit. He likes to listen to EDM, Hip-Hop, Rap, Pop, Rock, and occasionally classical. Tom also enjoys reading about car news and currently drives a Genesis Coupe and is hoping to one day own a BMW M2.
