Tom Hsu

Tom Hsu graduated from Rutgers University in 2012. After graduating, he worked at MRA International for 5 years. First starting in sales, he quickly moved to the role of an IT Technician. This involved repairing computers as well as imaging for schools. Eventually his role switched over to Website Administration, which involved working on the company's website and store front. He worked in both Magento 1 and 2, as well as WordPress. Currently, he has intermediate knowledge in HTML and CSS, and basic knowledge in JavaScript.

On his free time, he likes to play video games and watch movies. He is knowledgeable in headphones and owns multiple high end headphones: Fostex TH900 and Focal Elear. He also regularly uses the RHA T20 IEM's. His AMP and DAC are the following: Burson Soloist / Schiit BiFrost Multibit. Tom also enjoys reading about cars and drives a Genesis Coupe.
